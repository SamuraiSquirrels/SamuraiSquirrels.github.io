Chasing Ayaan: a blog about motherhood and toddlerhood

This blog is therefore dedicated to tackling every other child-rearing goal I've set out for myself; but most importantly, it'll be about understanding Ayaan's goals as he journeys through his toddler years.

My first entry will be about trying to create a Montessori/RIE environment in a one-bedroom apartment in the city.
